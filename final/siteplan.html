Site Purpose

A commerce site where users can create and purchase stickers customized to them based on their top albums and artists on spotify. The purpose is to provide a smooth user experience that feels very modern.

Target Audience

The target audience is anyone that listens to music! This site appeals to people that like to customize their belongings (laptops, watter bottles, etc.). This site also appeals to people to people looking to give personal gifts!
